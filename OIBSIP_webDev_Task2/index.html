Biography

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer known for his fierce passion, consistent performance, and dedication to the game. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. His journey began as a teenager with a dream, and he rose to become the captain of the Indian national team across formats.

Beyond his aggressive playing style and record-breaking centuries, Kohli is admired for his fitness, discipline, and leadership on and off the field. Under his captaincy, India achieved remarkable success, including historic wins overseas. He’s not just a cricketer; he’s an icon of modern India.
